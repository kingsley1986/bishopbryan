Bishop Solomon

R.V. PRABHU D O B 27- 03- 1964 born as Hindu in my young age very much devotee of Hindu God, Goddess by family follow up traditions, rituals Monday to Friday everyday visiting temples most part of Bangalore celebrating all Hindu festivals very particular and was following these idol worship.

I studied my high school in a Christian institution SEVENTH DAY ADVENTIST SCHOOL located in Bangalore during my school days started pilgrimage going sabarimala on the mountain place in KERALA our family very poor only person to earn my father alone private sector. During school days went ot sabrimala temple very famous season October to January every part of INDIA you can see people wearing black dress many rituals to follow as a) wear black dress, away from wife, eat food in banana leaf, sleep on floor matt,everyday going to temple doing banana, while pilgrimage in KERALA from particular place I walk 48 miles in wilderness very steep down and up, dancing on the way with colorful all over body midnight bath on dirty river too cold very chilled water. Before 1986 worked in many place traveling too for no proper food lot of friends was looking some for earning more money, many times attended Gospel meetings which takes place near by very interesting to hear about JESUS CHRIST IN FEAR I am only person in my class called Mala made out beeds around my neck and on forehead a Tilak. We were small family living in small rented house very difficult for luxurious survival on peace in home not able to come in life no lifting to higher standard during these situation fell in love with a young girl after few years got ditched my situation became worst only thought to suicide drank poison admitted in hospital police case was not well for few months could not come out of this problem it long time to settle. I had many health issue one day in 1986 went to visit visit one of my friend in his aunt's place he was not their his aunt saw me very young started telling JESUS CHRIST GOSPEL it touched me It touched my heart was very comforted with JESUS words she asked me come to church next day at evening 4 pm to her house to travel long distance, But my heart and mind says what will you do with one JESUS their are so many Hindu God and Goddess I left my my home evening around 5 passed 55 minutes decided to die on railway track as I was going on the way close to that aunts house some body diverting my direction speaking to my heart saying visit her it changed my way went at 6. 05 pm she was praying for me on her knees that God heard the prayer it changed and comforted me.

Even though late still went to church with her there a Pastor was praying in spirit I felt the presence of holy spirit got convinced since then started attending church regularly with lot opposition within family ,friends, neighbors but did not give up one year heard the Gospel in 1987 1st January took baptism in the NAME OF JESUS CHRIST acts 2:38 lot of prayers I got married in the year 4- 11- 1989 after one year son was born but earning was not enough tried going abroad was cheated by a friend but still tried and 1990 to 1994 worked in SAUDI ARABIA then returned worked with my Curzon brother in his company TAMIL NADU but always the desire to serve the LORD working doing ministry in 1999 the LORD made way asking me go back to Birth place BANGALORE continued my ministry with lot of opposition in the family everyday early morning live home return by night very tired my wife's complaint your parents are shouting because of you.

But continued my ministry in spite these inderrances 2003 my mother expired first fasting prayer buried her as per Hindu custom I did not perform any rituals did turn back the same year last fasting prayer of the year my father expired lot of rituals but my son and daughter were standing with me many relatives asked us to perform Hindu traditional rituals but never we turned up that his the time one of my relative question about Christianity abused me we here that you go everywere and speak about JESUS CHRIST and you lost your mother, father no one is there he cursed me saying you will start begging in the streets. I said till my salvation no God helped me in all situations but the GOD whom I serve now his the living God he will not let me down said as challenging till today OUR GOD JESUS CHRIST Blessed me pasturing small church with limited congregation many hurdles in ministry gave three place worship to other Pastors I have a small family myself, wife, Son Daughter both have done degree B C A Bachelor of computer and both are working still financially little bit suffering but by His grace living happily.
