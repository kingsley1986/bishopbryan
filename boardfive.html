Bishop Dr. Errol Sterling

Growing up in a single parent home, Reverend Dr. Errol Sterling, was molded with strong Christian values. His life story begins in Annotto Bay, St. Mary, Jamaica where he went to church with his mother.

In 1999 he walked in his divine calling as a Pastor. In that same year he attended Aenon Bible College and upon obtaining my Ministerial Certificate and was also ordained as a Pastor by the Pentecostal Assemblies of the World. After learning and accomplishing much, he founded the church he now pastors: True Light Apostolic Church Of Jesus Christ. After building his church within the will of God he was eager to learn more and started to attend Canadian West Indian Apostolic College where he received other Ministerial Certificates. In addition he attended the Philadelphia Christian Fellowship, he received even more Ministerial Certificates in the path of pursuing his dream as a Pastor.

Having a relationship with his congregation, he saw that they was a need for counselling, so he decided to gain further education to best aid his members. At the Canadian Christian Theological Seminary, in Calgary, Alberta he obtained his Bachelor and Master's Degree in Counselling and now operating his own Counselling Business, E.G.S Counselling Services as a Certified Clinical Counsellor. Reverend Sterling is a husband and a father of five. He is a humble servant of God who has a true calling on his life, to evangelize his christian beliefs by reaching lost souls for the Kingdom of God.
